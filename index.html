Kosmi Poker a way to create a private Poker room to play with your buddies!

This is how it works:
  1. You create a room with a shareable link.
  2. Share the link with your friends and play Texas Holdem together!
  3. Using WebRTC you can even communicate using webcam/microphone with members of the room and the members faces appear in their seats, making the experience feel just as if you are playing in real life!
