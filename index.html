SNES Party

Join us on Discord for finding players and more!: https://discord.gg/ZxV5N88

SNES Party is an experimental way to play NES games with your friends online all from within your browser using WebRTC.

This is how it works:
  1. You create a room with a shareable link.
  2. You or a member of the room starts a ROM in an Emulator running in the browser and thus becoming a host.
  3. The Video/Audio and Controls are streamed to the others in the room using WebRTC.
